🩺 Cancer Awareness & Early Detection

🔍 Common Cancer Symptoms

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue or weakness
  • Persistent cough or hoarseness
  • Unusual bleeding or discharge
  • Lumps or thickening in any area of the body
  • Changes in bowel or bladder habits
  • Skin changes, moles growing or bleeding

🧬 Common Types of Cancer

  • Skin Cancer – Often caused by sun exposure or UV radiation.
  • Brain Tumors – Can be malignant or benign and may affect memory, vision, or motor skills.
  • Lung Cancer – Strongly associated with smoking or pollution.
  • Breast, Prostate, Colon, and Cervical cancers are also among the most common worldwide.

🛡️ Prevention Tips

  • Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol intake
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Maintain a healthy weight and exercise regularly
  • Protect your skin from UV rays
  • Get vaccinated (e.g., HPV, Hepatitis B)
  • Get regular health check-ups and screenings

📅 Recommended Screening

  • Skin checks every 6–12 months (especially if you're at high risk)
  • Annual low-dose CT scan for smokers (lung cancer)
  • Routine brain MRIs if you experience neurological symptoms
  • Mammograms, colonoscopies, Pap tests depending on gender/age
